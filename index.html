VISIONARY SPORTS DEVELOPEMENT

LXLG is the Home of Champions, where champions are nurtured. We exist to contribute to the organisation of community, city and countrywide sports competitions, and provide a recreational utilization centre for families, students and corporate organisations.

Talent Development

We run various football academy and sports training programs to unearth raw talent, nurture and develop them into global sports icons.

Competition & Leagues

We organize various sports competitions to foster sports development and encourage youthful participation in sports across the country.

Sport Facilities

Our world class facilities include football pitches, swimming pools and ball courts, and will cater for any training session or event you have in mind.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Create an enabling environment for the provision of first football education and development program in first-class facilities

Produce footballers of exceptional quality through professional training

Educate our players about the opportunities available in soccer as a backup success plan in case soccer fails

Enlighten our graduates on the opportunities availablein soccer careers

Instill in our graduates a high sense of discipline, responsibilities, team-work and commitment
