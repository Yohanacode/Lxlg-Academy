WE ORGANIZE TOURNAMENTS

LXLG Soccer Academy is committed to promoting and developing the community through participating in professional soccer activities and providing a positive, supportive and safe environment where soccer players from diverse backgrounds are guided and inspired to reach their full potential both on and off field. We run football tournaments in communities across Kenya for the past ten years. Tournaments are a great way for teams to showcase their players and compete against the best teams in the region. We run tournaments in our local communities for many years. This helps support youth development through football. It is important for tournaments to give youth the opportunity to display their skills in a safe environment. It also gives us the opportunity to bring a positive message to many youth and work alongside local partners to tackle crime, poverty, and inequality.

WE HAVE INTERNATIONAL TRIPS AND TOURS

For the past two years LXLG Sports Academy has partnered with Football for Friendship to send youth to Spain for the International Training programs, where they are likey to get scouted.
