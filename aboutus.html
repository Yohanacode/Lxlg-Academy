ABOUT US

LXLG Sports Academy was founded from Lxlg sport after a series of organizing tournaments. The founder, Julio Odhiambo had a dream of setting up a place where the best players from the tournaments would be enrolled on a good training program with qualified coaches and better training types of equipment yo enable them better their skills. Also having grown up in the slums and manged tp play soccer to Nationwide league in the country, the director understood well about the vast talents in the country that needed to be tapped, mentored and nurtured for a better future regardless of their backgrounds and challenges.

OUR MISSION

We run various football academy and sports training programs to unearth raw talent, nurture and develop them into global sports icons.

OUR VISION

We organize various sports competitions to foster sports development and encourage youthful participation in sports across the country.

OUR VALUES

Our world class facilities include football pitches, swimming pools and ball courts, and will cater for any training session or event you have in mind.
